\begin{abstract}
\begin{fillbox}
Draft after results. Target beats: (1) fully GPU-resident scale-space blob
detector with no intermediate DoG volume in DRAM; (2) novel radial-flux
angular signature + coefficient-of-variation symmetry filter; (3) real-time
per-frame operation via persistent buffer reuse; (4) application to strain-field
capture from blob-marked skin over a flexing joint. State headline
speedup vs.\ PopSift/OpenCV-CUDA at equal repeatability, and strain-field
accuracy vs.\ ground truth.
\end{fillbox}
